This puzzle is made up of pairs of cards. The main body of this page shows the answer cards, while at the bottom is a single question card. Click on the correct answer card and it will turn over If you are correct the card will turn over to reveal the next question card. If Sudden Death is Off and you click on the wrong answer, a noise will sound. If you make a wrong choice when Sudden Death is On, the cards will dissolve and the game will reset itself.
