    2. How can you tell that a compound name will end in -ate?

      The formula ends in oxygen and contains 2 (or more) other elements.
    3. Why is MgO called magnesium oxide and not magnesium oxate?

      Compounds containing only two types of atoms end in -ide, not -ate.
