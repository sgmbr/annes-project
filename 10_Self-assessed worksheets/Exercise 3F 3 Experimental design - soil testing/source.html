3F 3 Experimental design: soil testing

Joe has just moved house. There is a rhododendron growing in a corner of the new section, where he intends to build a garage. He wants to move the bush to one of three other positions. The rhododendron is growing well in its present position, but Joe knows that rhododendrons need acid soil to grow well. He wants to know which of the three possible sites has the most acidic soil. The diagram below shows a method for testing the pH of soil.
  1. Joe needs to do a scientific experiment.
    1. State his aim.

      To find out which of the three possible sites has the most acidic soil

    2. Name the factor he will change during this experiment.

      The soil OR The place where the soil comes from.

    3. State the factors Joe must keep the same.

      Amount of soil (mass or volume); method used to collect the soil samples; volume and source of water used; time and method of shaking; amount and type of cotton wool used; type of universal indicator used.
      At least one of the factors in italics.
      At least three factors in total.

    4. What will Joe measure to find out the acidity of each soil sample?

      Its pH.

  2. Now write a simple set of instructions for Joe to carry out.

    • Take a heaped tablespoon of soil from about 4 cm below the surface of each site.
    • Crush the lumps in each sample and mix well, then take exactly 1/4 of a teaspoon of each soil and put in separate clean test tubes.
    • Add exactly 10 mL (a specified volume) of tap water to each tube.
    • Shake each for 10 s.
    • filter twice through 2 cm3 cotton wool.
    • Dip a small piece of universal test paper into the filtrate and compare the colour to the colour chart to find the pH.

    Method includes 2 of the bullet points above.
    Method includes 4 of the bullet points above.

  3. The universal test paper gives the pH of the filtrate. Explain how Joe can use this result to find out which soil is best for his rhododendron.

    The soil best for the rhododendron is the one with the lowest pH.
